My Tech and Life Journey

Learning, growing, and building—both in life and in tech.

By Adeboyejo Aliyah • January 19, 2026 • 5 min read

Life has been a journey of curiosity, learning, and self-discovery. From a young age, I’ve always been fascinated by how things work and how people think. That curiosity naturally led me into the world of tech, where I could create, explore, and solve problems in tangible ways. But my life outside tech is just as important. I value creativity, human connection, and personal growth, and these interests influence the projects I build and the impact I want to make in the world.

My Story

In the past few years, I’ve focused on learning frontend development and exploring innovative tech projects. Some highlights include:

  • AI Mental Wellness Platform: I designed a digital space where users can safely explore and understand their emotions. It’s a project close to my heart because it combines tech with mental health advocacy.
  • E-commerce Website ProjectBuilding a platform for an accessories store allowed me to apply both frontend and backend concepts in practical ways.
  • Preparing For First Jamb: I prepared for my first Jamb by studying hard and practicing consistently.
  • Multi-tasking between project from internships to apprenticeship to Studying: I was able to balance my time effectively and prioritize my tasks.

Life Outside Tech

While tech occupies a big part of my life, I also spend time nurturing my other passions:

  • Creative Design: Running my graphics design brand has allowed me to express myself visually and explore branding and storytelling.
  • Mental Health Awareness: I’m passionate about understanding human behavior and creating tools or projects that help people cope and grow.
  • Personal Growth: I enjoy reflecting, learning from experiences, and exploring hobbies that inspire curiosity and balance.

These experiences shape not just the projects I work on but also who I am as a person.

Moving Forward

My journey so far has been a blend of learning, challenges, and exciting discoveries. I’m motivated to continue growing, building meaningful projects, and making a positive impact through both tech and life experiences. Continuous Growth: Every project, experience, and hobby shapes not just my skills but also who I am as a person. They remind me that true growth comes from both professional achievements and personal reflection. Looking Ahead: I’m excited to keep exploring new ideas, learning from experiences, and creating projects that combine my passions for tech, creativity, and mental health awareness.
